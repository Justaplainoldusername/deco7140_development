Community Focus Area

I have chosen to focus on Hobbies and Subscultures as my Community Focus Area, and the hobby I have chosen is model boat building. The boat building community is diverse, but the hobby is often a solo activity, therefore I saw an opportunity to create community cohesion through the deployment of a website that fosters meaningful interactions.
