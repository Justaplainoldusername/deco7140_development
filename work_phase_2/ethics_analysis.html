You need to think more broadly than just breach of privacy - what actually might happen from the different uses of that data

o What data would you normally expect to share when signing up for a newsletter?

o What is being collected that is unexpected?

o Why is this data being collected? What are the possible consequences for people?

Now that you have identified the potential harms, you need to identify which ACS values and principles are relevant to those potential harms.

For each of the potential harms:

• Which Value is relevant? (Note there may be more than one)

• Identify the specific principle that is relevant - what should we doing

• Identify solutions (hint: the principle provides guidance for solutions)

How could you manage the tensions?

• What tensions might exist between you and your client?

• What options could you present to navigate these tensions
