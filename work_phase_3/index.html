A Website Showcasing the Style Guide

This website has been created to showcase our styling and design choices for the Connected Communities Initiative.

Our Community Focus Area is Hobbies and Subcultures, and our chosen community group is Model Boat Builders. This diverse community partake in a largely solo hobby, yet seek connection, collaboration and encouragement from others in their community. We beleive that a well-designed website that nurtures interactivity and meaningful connections will be of great support to this community.
