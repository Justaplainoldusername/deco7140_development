Site Map

Here you will find our proposal for the overall structure of the website. We have aimed to acheive a structure that is as flat as possible, will still maintaining a natural hierarchy that users will be familiar with. Titles in a green box are for navigation purposes only and will not be represented by pages within the website.

The About section offers new visitors an opportunity tolearn about the community and the website. It will not be a page in the website. Rather, it will be a drop-down item accessible from the navigation bar that will be available throughout the website.

Structured like an About Us page, content within the page will serve the general public and curious newcomers. Visitors will be able to read answers to common questions learn about diversity within the community and be introduced to the unique characteristics of the Community.

The page will contain links to featured Stories submitted by the Community, as well as a link to the Newsletter Subscription Form and the Signup Form which will allow them to partake in the Discussion topics.

Not only is the intent for the website to be an inclusive and welcoming space that minimises harm, but it must also remain a space that is free from commercial activity. To achieve these goals, we have included a page devoted to the Terms of Use of the website that will guide users in ensuring the good intentions of the website are upheld.

The Stories section will contain a library of stories related to model boat building submitted by the community on the themes of Personal Stories, Fiction, and Tales from History. The section will provide the community with a platform from which to promote diversity within the community share their personal stories and enjoy the romance and creativity that comes with model boat building. It will not be a page in the website. Rather, it will be a drop-down item accessible from the navigation bar that will be available throughout the website.

This page will display the website's library of personal stories submitted by members of the website community. Much like a bookshelf, links to the stories will be displayed as rows and columns of cards with a supporting image and text containing the story title, the author, and an excerpt from the story. The four featured stories will appear at the top of the page and will be rotated periodically as new content is submitted. A link to the Submit a Story page will be included, as too will the Newsletter Subscription Feature to encourage uptake of the newsletter.

Our users show a preference for mouse-free navigation, therefore cards will be large, clickable across their entire field, and accessible with hover and focus features to make navigation as straightforward as possible. This will also improve navigation on a mobile or smaller devices.

Each story will have its own page for maximum readability and to ensure there is anough real-estate on the page for any images accompanying the story. However, a side bar will be employed to promote feature or related stories and recent activity from the site to help encourage further engagement. A comments section will be included below the story to encourage interaction and engagement with the story. Wherever user interaction is possible within the site we will include a flag system to prevent against harmful use of the site or the presence of commercial activity.

Our users displayed an appreciation for the imaginary elements of their hobby and this section of the site will be devoted to the enjoyment and sharing of Fictional stories related to boat building or nautical life. It will follow the same structure as Personal Stories.

This page will follow the same structure at Story (Personal)

Model boat building often strikes a balance between creating something attractive and adhering to historical accuracy. It is often the history of nautical travel that attracts people to the craft, therefore we wish to create a space for sharing on this theme also. It will follow the same structure as Personal Stories.

This page will follow the same structure at Story (Personal)

A digital story submission form on this page will enable our community to share their stories and represent the diversity and uniqueness of the community.

During our user interviews in Work Phase 2, the community shared their need for access to quality resources that will assist their build projects. This section will be dedicated to the curation of a library of resources such as videos, images and guides that will benefit our community of model boat builders. As with the About Us section, Resources will be a menu item, not a page.

This page will contain a grid of cards that link to their dedicate build kit page. The appearance and functional will be identical to Personal Stories, where users can click anywhere on the card to navigate to the page for easy mouse-free navigation.

From the Kit Page, users will be able to find resources related to that particular kit. These resources may be images, and we will provide interactive galleries and embedded videos that allow users to view high resolution media to support their hobby.

As the library grows and develop, these pages may evolved to include subpages, therefore a breadcrumb menu will be included to assist navigation.

In Work Phase 2 our community expressed a need for resources dedicated to common techniques, particulary for new builders. This page will draw from the same layout as Personal Stories and Kit Specific Guides to ensure consistency across the site.

Each page will focus the main content on the featured technique. A side bar will contain links to similiar techniques and recent activity on the site. A comments section below the main content will allow users to offer feedback and additional suggestions. As this page features interactivity, there will be a flag system for misuse or commercial activity.
