Behavioural Design

Link Styles

During Work Phase 2, we uncovered a user preference for mouse-free navigation. To support this, we have developed a link style that advertises its clickability through features such as a drop shadow which imitates a 3d button in real life, hover effects that indicate interactabilty, and the button-like icon of a stylised compass that symbolises navigation. While the icon appears to be the click target, the entire field has been activated as a link to increase the usability of this component. Four variations have been provided to maximise the versatility of this feature.
